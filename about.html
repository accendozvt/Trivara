Our Story

Who We Are

At Trivara Staffing we connect businesses with exceptional talent across healthcare, hospitality, construction, and global workforce placement. We specialise in delivering flexible, compliant staffing solutions and opening doors for professionals seeking meaningful, career-advancing opportunities. Driven by quality, reliability, and a people-first approach, we're building a strong reputation as a trusted partner for both employers and candidates.

Founded on the belief that the right hire changes everything, Trivara was built to bridge the gap between ambitious organisations and skilled professionals who are ready to make a genuine impact. Our team brings years of hands-on recruitment experience across multiple sectors, giving us the insight to move quickly without sacrificing quality.

Compliance is not an afterthought for us, it is embedded in everything we do. From HIQA-aligned healthcare placements to fully documented overseas recruitment programmes, we ensure every candidate we place meets the regulatory and professional standards your organisation demands. You can trust that Trivara has done the groundwork so that you don't have to.

Whether you are a nursing home, healthcare provider, or care facility searching for experienced nurses and healthcare assistants, a hotel group in need of dependable hospitality staff, or a growing construction firm that requires reliable tradespeople at short notice, Trivara delivers. We operate Ireland-wide and draw on international talent pipelines to ensure no staffing challenge is ever out of reach.
