Our Process

How We Work

A clear, proven process that delivers results — whether you need local cover or international talent.

Agency Placements

1

Understand Your Needs

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your staffing requirements, organisational culture, compliance obligations, and timeline — ensuring we find candidates who truly fit.

2

Source & Screen

Our experienced team draws on an extensive talent network and active database to source candidates. Each applicant undergoes rigorous reference checks, skills assessment, and compliance verification.

3

Match & Present

We present a shortlist of thoroughly vetted candidates tailored to your role, along with detailed profiles. You make the final decision — we handle coordination and scheduling.

4

Placement & Support

Once placed, we remain your point of contact. We follow up to ensure the placement is working well for both parties and provide ongoing support for the duration of the engagement.

Overseas Recruitment

1

Initial Assessment

We assess your workforce gap, identify the most suitable sourcing regions, and establish a clear plan covering timelines, costs, compliance requirements, and OET (Occupational English Test) preparation guidance for healthcare professionals.

2

Global Talent Search

Leveraging our international recruitment network, we identify and screen qualified candidates from key talent markets. All candidates are assessed against your exacting standards before shortlisting.

3

Visa & Compliance

We manage all immigration documentation, work permit applications, and regulatory compliance requirements — reducing administrative burden and ensuring a smooth, lawful transition for every candidate.

4

Arrival & Integration

We support candidates through relocation, induction, and early integration — ensuring they settle into their new roles and environments effectively. We remain a resource for both employer and employee throughout.
