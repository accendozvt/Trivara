For agency placements, we can often provide same-day or next-day cover depending on availability and sector. We maintain an active pool of pre-vetted, compliance-ready professionals.

Agency staffing covers temporary, flexible, or short-notice cover where we employ and supply the worker. Permanent placement is a direct hire service, where we recruit and place a candidate who becomes your full-time employee.

All candidates undergo identity verification, professional reference checks, qualification validation, Garda vetting (where applicable), and sector-specific compliance checks aligned with HIQA and other regulatory standards.

Yes. Our healthcare placements are aligned with HIQA standards. We ensure all placed healthcare professionals hold current registrations, required training certificates, and are fully vetted before deployment.

OET (Occupational English Test) is an English proficiency test specifically designed for healthcare professionals. It is required by the Irish Nursing and Midwifery Board (NMBI) for nurses and HCAs registering to work in Ireland from non-English-speaking countries.

Yes. For overseas recruits, we manage the full documentation process including work permit applications, NMBI registration support (for nurses), immigration coordination, and pre-arrival compliance checks.
